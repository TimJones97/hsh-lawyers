About Us

Experience. Innovation. Solutions.

Our firm was founded by an experienced Criminal Defence Lawyer with over 50 years experience in Criminal Law.

We aim to provide legal services in a timely and cost effective manner. When you have a problem, we provide advice and representation to achieve the best possible outcome.

Based in Maroochydore, we are able, with the assistance of our sister firm and affiliates to assist you with all matters in Queensland.

Committed to our clients and driven by experience.

At HSH Lawyers, we have an approachable team of lawyers and conveyancers who are highly experienced in relation to all type of matters undertaken by the firm from Conveyancing, Wills and Estates, Business & Commercial Law, and Powers of Attorney.

We have seen firsthand the benefits that a client receives, including reduced anxiety and stress, when they have been properly informed of likely outcomes throughout their matters.

We are committed to our clients and driven by experience and we find the best solution to your problems.

We have affilliations with a number of other firms on the Sunshine Coast and Brisbane. If we are unable to assist you with your legal matter, one of our affiliated firms is bound to be able to assist.
